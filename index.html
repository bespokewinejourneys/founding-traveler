The Georgia Experience

This inaugural trip explores Georgia’s living wine culture through a limited, intentionally paced journey designed for travelers who value depth over volume. Wine is the lens — culture is the destination.

  • Small group (8–10 guests)
  • Slow, immersive pace
  • Personal access to producers
  • No mass tourism, no rushed itineraries
  • Designed to function at a human scale
